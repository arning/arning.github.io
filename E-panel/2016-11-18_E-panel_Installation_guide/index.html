Mini-PC installation guide

Required items

  • Mini-PC
  • USB AC adapter
  • USB cable
  • HDMI cable
  • Duct tape

Completed installation

Installation

  1. Plug-in the AC adapter.

  2. Connect the USB cable and the AC adapter.

  3. Lead the USB cable behind the monitor fixing plate.

  4. Connect HDMI cable and Stick-PC

  5. Connect Stick-PC and Monitor by the HDMI cable.

  6. Connect USB cable and the Stick-PC.

  7. Fix the Stick-PC and the USB cable securely by duct tape.

  8. Keep the ventilation slots open!
